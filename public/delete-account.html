Delete Your Account & Data

Design Closet · Last updated 15 July 2026

You can request permanent deletion of your Design Closet account and all associated data at any time. This page explains what is deleted and how to request it — no app install required.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and sign-in credentials
  • All wardrobe items and their photos
  • Your saved outfits, events, and wear history
  • Your style profile (name, palette, sizes, goals, preferences)

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Calendar data is never stored by us, so there is nothing to delete there.

How to request deletion

  1. Tap the button below (or email us at jolman009@gmail.com).
  2. Send the request from the email address associated with your account, so we can verify it’s you.
  3. We will permanently delete your account and data within 30 days and confirm by email.
Request account deletion

If the button doesn’t open your mail app, email jolman009@gmail.com with the subject “Account deletion request” and include your account email.

Data retention

Once processed, your personal data is removed from our active systems. Residual copies in encrypted backups are purged on our normal backup rotation (within 30 days). We may retain minimal records only where required by law.
