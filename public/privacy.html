Privacy Policy

Design Closet · Last updated 15 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Design Closet (“we”, “us”, the “App”) collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. Design Closet is a personal wardrobe styling app that suggests outfits from the clothes you own. By using the App you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

AccountYour email address and a securely hashed password, to create and access your account.
Wardrobe dataDetails you add about your clothing (name, brand, category, colors, fabric, season, formality, warmth, favorites), your saved outfits, events you create, wear history, and profile details (display name, optional location, style persona, palette, sizes, goals).
PhotosImages of your clothing that you choose to upload. Photos are resized on your device before upload.
Approximate locationIf you grant permission, your device’s coordinates are used once to fetch local weather. We do not store your location. If you decline, a default city is used.
Google Calendar (optional)If you connect Google Calendar, we read your upcoming events (read-only) to display them and plan outfits. Calendar data is used only during your session and is not stored in our database.
Device storageWe store app preferences and sign-in session data locally on your device (e.g., theme choice, offline cache) so the App works smoothly and offline.

How we use your information

  • To provide the core service: organizing your closet and generating outfit suggestions.
  • To detect clothing attributes from a photo when you enable Smart Tagging (see “AI processing” below).
  • To show weather-appropriate suggestions and plan outfits for your events.
  • To keep your account secure and operate the App reliably.

We do not use advertising, ad networks, or third-party analytics/tracking SDKs. We do not sell your personal information.

AI processing (Smart Tagging)

When you turn on Smart Tagging and add a photo, that image is sent through our secure server function to Anthropic’s Claude model to identify attributes (category, colors, fabric, etc.). Anthropic processes the image to return these tags and does not use API inputs to train its models. If you keep Smart Tagging off, photos are analyzed only on your device.

Service providers

We rely on the following processors to run the App. Your data is shared with them only as needed to provide the service, and they are bound by their own privacy commitments:

SupabaseDatabase, authentication, and photo storage. Your wardrobe data and photos are stored here, isolated to your account.
AnthropicAI photo tagging (only when you use Smart Tagging).
Google FirebaseWeb hosting for the App.
GoogleCalendar access, only if you connect it.
Open-MeteoWeather data (receives approximate coordinates, no account info).

Data retention & your choices

  • You can edit or delete individual items, outfits, and events at any time in the App.
  • You can disconnect Google Calendar at any time from the Events screen.
  • You can request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data at any time via our account deletion page.
  • You may also request access to or correction of your data by contacting us (below). We will honor applicable rights under laws such as the GDPR and CCPA.

Security

Access to your data is protected by per-user security rules, and connections use encryption in transit (HTTPS). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Children

Design Closet is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
