## Full description (≤4000 chars)
Design Closet is your personal stylist in your pocket. Snap photos of the clothes you already own, and let Design Closet build a digital wardrobe that suggests what to wear — every day, for any occasion, in seconds.

No more staring at a full closet with "nothing to wear." Design Closet learns your pieces and puts them together into looks that fit the weather, your schedule, and your style.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

• Daily outfit suggestion — Open the app to a look picked for today, factoring in the live weather and your upcoming plans.

• Your digital closet — A beautiful, photo-first grid of everything you own. Filter by category, mark favorites, and find any piece in a tap.

• Smart Tagging (AI) — Add a photo and let AI detect the category, colors, fabric, and more, so cataloging your wardrobe takes seconds instead of minutes.

• Outfit generator — Choose an occasion (Work, Date Night, Casual, Formal, Active, or Travel) and instantly get curated looks scored on formality, warmth, season, and your favorite colors. Save the ones you love.

• Plan for events — Add events, or connect Google Calendar, and plan a polished outfit for each one ahead of time.

• Wear tracking — Log what you wear so your suggestions rotate fresh, and rediscover great pieces you've been overlooking.

• Style profile — Set your persona, preferred palette, sizes, and style goals to make every suggestion more "you."

• Two aesthetics — Switch between a soft, minimalist boutique theme and a refined charcoal-and-bronze look.

BUILT TO FEEL EFFORTLESS

Design Closet is fast, works offline, and installs like a native app. There are no ads and no third-party trackers — your closet is yours. Your wardrobe and photos are stored securely and kept private to your account.

WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT

• Get dressed faster every morning
• Actually wear more of what you own
• Pack smarter for trips and events
• Shop with intention — see the gaps in your wardrobe
• Bring order and calm to getting ready

Whether you're building a capsule wardrobe, planning outfits for a busy week, or just tired of the daily "what should I wear?", Design Closet turns your existing clothes into effortless, confident style.

Add a few pieces and your stylist takes it from there.
